City Hospital's Financial and Operational Analysis

This project involved a comprehensive analysis of City Hospital's financial and operational data to provide a centralized view of their performance. The goal was to enable data-driven decision-making by identifying trends, optimizing resource allocation, and improving financial strategies. I Successfully created a centralized view of the hospital's financial performance, enabling better visibility into key financial metrics, provided insights into revenue generation trends, helping the hospital understand their financial trajectory over time, identified high and low revenue-generating specialties and procedures, assisting in resource optimization and strategic planning, highlighted top revenue-generating doctors and patients, enabling targeted efforts to enhance revenue, delivered detailed analyses of doctor and patient distributions across specialties, as well as patient visit trends, aiding in operational decision-making, and offered a gender-based breakdown of doctors and patients, supporting diversity and inclusion initiatives within the hospital, This Analysis was performed using Microsoft Power Bi.

Guyus Real Estate Using Microsost Power Bi

This Real Estate Company got in contact with us because they were experiencing Delayed Decision Making, Data Integrity Issues, Limited Analytical Capability, and Inefficiency in Reporting. Due to the usage of Microsoft Excell to Compile, analyze, and report its cumbersome monthly sales and transaction data, After analysis, we successfully implemented Power BI to automate data reporting processes, enabling real-time data visualization and insights, developed a detailed data model encompassing various dimensions of the business, including transactions, properties, agents, and locations, to structure data for analysis and reporting, consolidated data from multiple sources into a unified Power BI platform, ensuring data integrity and consistency across reports, and leveraged Power BI's advanced analytical tools to uncover market trends, performance metrics, and actionable insights, facilitating informed strategic decision-making for Guyus Real Estate..
